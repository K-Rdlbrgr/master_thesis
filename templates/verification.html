TWO FACTOR AUTHENTICATION

Two-factor authentication adds an additional layer of security to the authentication process by making it harder for attackers to gain access to a person's online accounts. We implemented it by generating an individual password for you in Step 1. To provide the highest possible privacy for your vote, your password is not being stored in our database and will not be accessible anymore once you left this page. Please make sure you copied it and save it somewhere on your device in order to verify your vote later.
